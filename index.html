Overview

A structured path into English reading

Who it is for

Young learners who are developing literacy in English as a foreign language, with materials designed for school and home.

What it aims to do

Introduce phonics in a clear sequence so children can decode, read aloud, and build comprehension with growing independence.

How it feels

Warm, accessible, and calm. The concept uses dyslexia-friendly typography and strong visual clarity rather than busy classroom graphics.
